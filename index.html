Biography

Rui Li is a Algorithm Enginner II in Alibaba Cloud. He received his M.S under the supervision of Prof. Shu-Tao Xia and Prof. Xi Xiao from the department of computer science and technology at Tsinghua University in 2019. And he received his B.S degree in Computer Science from Nankai University in 2016.

His current research interest is to improve reliability and effectiveness of large-scale systems and networks through machine learning methods(e.g., NLP, time-series analysis, log analysis). He used to focus on network traffic classification when he was in Tsinghua University.
